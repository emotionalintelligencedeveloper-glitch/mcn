People-Pleaser Recovery — A Gentle Reset

This tool helps you notice where you say yes too quickly, hide your needs, or feel responsible for others’ emotions — and guides you toward safer, healthier self-respect.

1) Where do you often say yes even when you want to say no?

2) What are you afraid might happen if you disappoint someone?

3) How does people-pleasing affect your energy or mood?

4) What do you usually put aside to keep others comfortable?

5) What is one small boundary you could practice this week?
