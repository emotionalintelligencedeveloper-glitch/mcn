Boundary Builder — A Gentle Self-Respect Tool

This tool helps you notice where your boundaries feel stretched, unclear, or crossed — and guides you toward calmer, healthier limits without guilt or confrontation.

1) Where do you most often feel drained, resentful, or uncomfortable?

2) What do people expect from you in this situation?

3) What do you actually need or want instead?

4) What makes it hard for you to say no or speak up?

5) What would a respectful boundary sound like in simple words?
