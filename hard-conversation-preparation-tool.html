Hard Conversation Preparation Tool

This gentle tool helps you prepare for a difficult conversation — emotionally, practically, and safely. You don’t need perfect words. You just need a starting point.

Important:
If this conversation involves abuse, threats, or danger, your safety matters more than saying the “right” thing. You are allowed to delay, leave, or seek help first.

1) Who is this conversation with?

2) What do you want to say but feel unable to?

3) What are you most afraid might happen?

4) What truth or boundary matters most here?

5) What outcome would feel safe enough — not perfect?

6) If this conversation goes badly, who or what can support you?
