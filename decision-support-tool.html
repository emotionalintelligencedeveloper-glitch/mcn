I’m Stuck — Decision Support Tool

This tool is for moments when your mind feels full, your emotions are loud, and making a decision feels overwhelming. You don’t need to decide everything — just the next gentle step.

Reminder: Feeling stuck does not mean you are failing. It often means you care — and your nervous system needs clarity, not pressure.

1) What decision or situation are you feeling stuck on right now?

2) What emotion feels loudest when you think about this?

3) What are you most afraid might happen if you choose “wrong”?

4) If you did nothing for another week, how would that affect you?

5) What option feels slightly safer or lighter — even if imperfect?
