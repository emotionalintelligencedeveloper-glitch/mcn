Domestic Violence Warning & Safety Tool

This tool is a quiet, private space to reflect. If something in your relationship feels confusing, frightening, or controlling, you deserve clarity and safety — without pressure or judgment.

If you are in immediate danger:
Please contact local emergency services or a trusted person right now. This tool is for reflection and support, not emergency response.

1) Do you ever feel afraid of how someone might react to you?

2) Have you felt controlled, monitored, or isolated?

3) Do arguments leave you feeling confused, ashamed, or blamed?

4) Have you changed your behaviour to avoid upsetting someone?

5) What worries you most about your current situation?
