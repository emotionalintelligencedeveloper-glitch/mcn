Gun Violence Awareness & Emotional Support

If you’re worried about gun violence — whether for yourself, someone you know, or a situation that feels unsafe — this tool helps you slow down, reflect, and choose safer next steps.

If there is immediate danger:
Please contact local emergency services or a trusted authority right now. This tool supports prevention and awareness, not crisis response.

1) What made you seek support today?

Understanding the trigger helps clarify next steps.

2) Who are you most concerned about?

Yourself, someone else, or a situation you’ve noticed.

3) What warning signs or behaviors have you noticed?

Changes in mood, talk of harm, isolation, anger, fear, or fixation.

4) What emotions are you experiencing right now?

Fear, confusion, anger, helplessness, concern — all are valid.

5) What support or safety step feels possible today?

A conversation, creating distance, seeking professional help.
